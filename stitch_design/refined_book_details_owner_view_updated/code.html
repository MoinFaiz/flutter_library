Whispers of the Ancient Vellum

Elias Thorne

ENGLISH LITERATURE HISTORY PHILOSOPHY

Archival Summary

An exhaustive study of pre-Renaissance manuscripts, Whispers of the Ancient Vellum delves into the tactile history of the written word. Elias Thorne masterfully weaves together chemical analysis of inks with the sociopolitical climate that birthed these fragile survivors of history. This edition serves as the definitive owner's reference for the 14th-century Oxford collection.
